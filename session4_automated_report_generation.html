AI-Assisted Data Analysis with Cursor

Go from raw data to a publication-quality plot in minutes.

Step 1: The Initial Prompt

Provide Cursor's AI chat with a clear, natural language instruction about your data and goal.

"Please first help simulate some reaction_kinetics datasets just for demo and save it then I have a CSV file named 'reaction_kinetics.csv' with columns 'time_s', 'concentration_A', and 'temperature_C'. Write a Python script using pandas and matplotlib to load this data and create a plot of concentration_A versus time_s."

Step 2: Refining the Plot

The initial plot is a great start. Now, ask for professional refinements.

"That's great. Now, make the plot look more professional. Add a grid, make the line dashed and blue, and save the plot to a file called 'kinetics_plot.png' instead of showing it."

Step 3: The Final Output

Cursor modifies the code and produces a publication-quality visualization based on your prompts.

Deep Research with Google Gemini

Turn results into contextual understanding and accelerate literature review.

Step 1: The Experimental Finding

Distill your key results into a concise summary.

"Our experiment on a new perovskite-based catalyst for ammonia synthesis showed a 92% yield in 6 hours at 80°C and 1 atm. The reaction kinetics appear to follow a second-order rate law. The catalyst showed no signs of degradation after 24 hours of continuous operation."

Step 2: The Prompt for Gemini

Craft a detailed prompt asking Gemini to find conclusions, search literature, and suggest next steps. The JSON format ensures a structured, predictable response.

Based on the following chemistry research results, please perform the following tasks and structure your response in a single JSON object with the keys "conclusions", "related_literature", and "next_steps": 1. **conclusions**: Analyze the results and provide three insightful conclusions a researcher could draw. 2. **related_literature**: Use Google Search to find two recent scientific papers that report similar findings with perovskite catalysts for ammonia synthesis. For each paper, provide the title, authors, and a one-sentence summary. 3. **next_steps**: Suggest three specific, actionable next steps for further research based on these findings. **Research Results:** "Our experiment on a new perovskite-based catalyst for ammonia synthesis showed a 92% yield in 6 hours at 80°C and 1 atm. The reaction kinetics appear to follow a second-order rate law. The catalyst showed no signs of degradation after 24 hours of continuous operation."

Step 3: Gemini's Grounded Response

Gemini returns a structured JSON with insightful conclusions, relevant literature, and clear suggestions for future experiments.

{ "conclusions": [ "The perovskite catalyst exhibits exceptionally high catalytic activity for ammonia synthesis at unusually low temperatures and atmospheric pressure, challenging traditional high-pressure paradigms.", "The material's high stability over a 24-hour period suggests it is a robust candidate for long-term industrial applications.", "The second-order rate law provides a crucial clue for mechanistic studies, implying a reaction mechanism that likely involves two molecules of a reactant species in the rate-determining step." ], "related_literature": [ { "title": "A Ruthenium-Based Perovskite Electrocatalyst for Ammonia Synthesis...", "authors": "Wang, J., et al.", "summary": "The study presents a ruthenium-doped perovskite that achieves high Faradaic efficiency for ammonia synthesis at ambient conditions." } ], "next_steps": [ "Conduct in-situ spectroscopic analysis (e.g., DRIFTS, XPS) to elucidate the reaction mechanism and identify the active sites on the perovskite surface.", "Perform long-term stability tests (over 500 hours) to confirm the catalyst's industrial viability.", "Investigate the effect of doping the perovskite with other transition metals to potentially enhance the reaction rate." ] }

Build Your Own AI Assistant Workflow

Apply the techniques from the demos to a chemistry topic of your choice. This exercise will help solidify your understanding and show you the power of these tools on your own research problems.

Task 1: Data to Plot with Cursor

  1. Select a topic and create a small, simple CSV file with realistic-looking data.
  2. Use Cursor's AI Chat to write a Python script that loads your data, creates a professional plot, and calculates a meaningful metric (e.g., average yield).
  3. Ask Cursor to make at least one refinement to your plot.

Task 2: Results to Context with Gemini

  1. Write a one-sentence summary of the result from your Python script.
  2. Use the demo's Python script as a template to query Gemini for a summary, related phenomena, and search keywords.
  3. Analyze the output. Were the suggestions helpful for your topic?
